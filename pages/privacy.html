Privacy Policy

ScaffoldBot is an educational AI tool designed to support teachers with lesson scaffolding, especially for ELL/ESL learners. We respect your privacy and are committed to keeping your data safe.

What We Collect

ScaffoldBot does not collect personal information such as names, emails, or student data. You should never enter sensitive or personally identifiable information into the tool.

How Data Is Used

Prompts you enter are sent to OpenAI’s API in order to generate responses. These prompts and responses may be used by OpenAI for system monitoring and improvement. ScaffoldBot itself does not store or share your inputs.

Children’s Data

ScaffoldBot is intended for teacher use. It should not be used by students directly, and no student names or details should ever be entered.

Third-Party Services

ScaffoldBot relies on OpenAI’s GPT technology. Please review OpenAI’s Terms of Use and Privacy Policy for more information on how your data is processed.

Your Responsibilities

As an educator, you are responsible for ensuring that no personally identifiable student data is entered into the system. Always review AI outputs for accuracy and appropriateness before using them in class.
